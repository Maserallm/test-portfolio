Maserall's Top Anime Shows of All Time!

My favorite anime shows are:

Hunter x Hunter

This is a great show about the adventure of a young boy who is seeking to be reunited with his father whom he has inherited keen hunting characteristics that enable him to hunt down danger

Cowboy Bebop

Space cowboy trying to find his one true love again only to be met with epic tragedy from old friends turned enemies

Dragon Ball Super

The story of Goku and his friends trying to attain new levels of strength and mastery with their martial arts skills as new foes appear from galaxies away
